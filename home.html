About Us

Jawaan Jai Kisaan given by the three P.M Lal Bahadur Sastri. This implies the importance of the two forces for improvement of a nation.Our present concept is about kisaan i.e; the farmer with whom sweating every person in the nation get his food.


So, a farmer plays a pivotal role in producing the adequate food grains to cater to the needs of the country So, it is the primary responsibility of the GOVT to give let mort priority to the field of agriculture and the full support to middle and small scale farmer in producing the harvest but the GOVT efforts are not fulfilling the needs of small scale farmers and hence they are facing financial problems in cultivation.

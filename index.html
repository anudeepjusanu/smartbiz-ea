About Us

Rythu Rajyam is a charitable trust established in the year 2018 by V Srinivas and V Saroja Devi and decided to serve for below poverty line, ex-servicemen, Farmers.

The principal objective of the Trust is to provide interest free loans and hassle-free procurement.

Services

Inspired from the Slogan of JAI JAWAN JAI KISAN,We feel Responsible to help farmers with the following initiatives

CROP INSURANCE

Crop insurance to protect against either the loss of their crops due to natural disasters.

FERTILIZERS

Ensure essential nutrients for growth.

PROCUREMENT

Ensure that farmers get remunerative prices.

Vision and Mission

Mission of trust is support financially without any interest and to provide services to farmers like cultivation, Fertilizers, pesticides and provision of procurement and supply of all the farmer products(Food Grains, vegetables) from the farmers and to the farmers. Make the farmer happy with all facilities for cultivation. Rythu Rajyam trust should be one of the fastest growing farmers organization where as all the farmers in AP will make the Rythu Rajyam as a unique
